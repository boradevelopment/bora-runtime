# Code of Conduct 
## This code of conduct is applied through all public repositories inside of the boradevelopment organization

### What you can do
* You are free to say whatever you want, swear words will not be condemned in the community and honestly, as long as it's hurting nobody. Who even cares

### What you can't do
* Harass member's or contributors in the community, this can cause drama so please keep it peace.
* Show any forms of bigotry against a group, person, etc. Be fair to everybody.
* Spread misinformation or lie in the community about certain topics, like features, bugs or etc.
